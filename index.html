We aspire towards a more equal world.

One where people are able to build livelihoods, lead a life of dignity, ensure good health, secure a better standard of living for their families and feel equal and capable of contributing to their communities and driving change. All while nurturing nature and all forms of life around us.

Work with Us

We Look To Achieve Impact Primarily Through

Release strategic grants, usually for specific projects and program-related investment
Provide resources and tools to grantees, helping them create a larger impact with their work
Impact investments, either program-related or mission-related, to invest in or partner with social entrepreneurs.
Building partnerships with like-minded organizations which offer different perspectives and expertise

Focus Areas

Sustainable Livelihood

Create opportunities for building livelihoods and mainstream careers through skilling programs and extending reach for the less privileged.

Innovation in Social Impact

Fund unique, scalable solutions that can create jobs and multiply efforts in rural education and healthcare; as well as revive traditional crafts

Education & Sport

Address the deficit of opportunities faced by rural and differently-abled children in the realm of education and sports through sponsor programs. Identify key talent who can leverage support extended by the foundation to achieve their potential.

Community Development

Expand TEF’s partner network to build awareness, capabilities and skills amongst rural women and through them enhance the communities they work with. How we support our partners

People, Animals and Nature

Extending grants, resources and our network towards initiatives like the Animal Shelter at PFA (People for Animals)

How We Support Our Partners

Our focus is on enabling causes, projects and entrepreneurial ventures innovating to create a more equal world by providing guidance, financial support and opening up networks and partnerships for leverage
